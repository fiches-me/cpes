# Appeals in advertisement
*How to get an ad to stand out?* Use **appeals** to grab the viewer's attention.
## Emotional
1. Personal appeals
   -> THE Emotional appeal
   -> Gillette & sandpaper
2. Social appeals (IKEA)
   -> Captures popular trends
   -> Game Of Thrones, "winter collection is coming", IKEA
3. Fear appeals
   -> Strong visuals
   -> SOS Brazilia
4. Humour appeals
   -> Humour is subjective, warning!
   -> Virgin Media
5. Celebrities appeals
   -> Use influence from huge names.
   -> Snickers
6. Sexual appeals
   -> Attractive models & sexy product shots
   -> Hardy's, Victoria Secrets
7. Romantic appeals
   -> Powerful inspire feelings & nostalgia
   -> Parfums
8. Youth appeals
   -> older generations
   -> Centrum Silver & Bruss Willis
9. Adventure appeals
   -> Where the product can take us. "What if" feeling
   -> Jeep
10. Popularity appeals
   -> Dah
   -> Baywatch Slow Mo Marathon
11. Musical appeals
   -> music must complement setting (basic cinema theory)
   -> At the end, it's a plus
12. Eloathy Appeals
   -> Tricky Technic, to identify a problem they never had before, use by public services/philanthropy
   -> Safe at home, 
13. Potential appeals (what *could* be)
   -> Potential of kids
   -> Lego

   -> Sell for higher cost, for a social status.
